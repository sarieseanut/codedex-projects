Welcome to sarie—space!

I've taken my time to create a lil space for me to freely express myself in my life. I've gone from sketching on notebooks and schoolbooks, to writing stories and poetry. From making music to creating playlists that elicit feelings and memories. I've even explored cooking up recipes that excite my taste buds, and painting pictures that reflect the universe in my mind. I hope to be able to share any and all of the art that I've created and maybe touch a soul or two. May the infinite universes my mind and hands have created inspire you.





About Me

Stephanie is a twenty-something working zillennial with a career background in project management, accounts management, and sales. During her spare time, she finds ways to keep herself occupied with activities. She enjoys reading, writing stories, painting, binge-watching series, and learning new things.



You can learn more about her on her Notion site.
